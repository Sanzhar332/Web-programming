Behind the Scenes

"Interstellar" is known for its exceptional production and scientific accuracy. Here's a closer look at the making of the film:

  • The movie's visual effects were groundbreaking, with a focus on real science. The depiction of the black hole, Gargantua, was based on actual physics equations.
  • The crew constructed practical spaceship sets, such as the Endurance spacecraft, to immerse the actors in a realistic environment.
  • Hans Zimmer's mesmerizing score, featuring a church organ, added a unique and emotional dimension to the film.
